# Wallis (VS) – Official Sources

## Tariff & Calculation
- **StG VS**: Legal basis
- **Kantonsblatt ESTV**: https://www.estv2.admin.ch/stp/kb/vs-de.pdf

## Key Parameters
- 3-tier progressive: ≤50k → 12%, 50k–100k → 18%, >100k → 24%
- Minimum tax: CHF 100 (if <100, not collected)
- Surcharge for <5yr: multiplied rate (yr1: ×1.6, yr2: ×1.45, yr3: ×1.3, yr4: ×1.15, yr5: ×1.0)
- Discount: 4%/yr from yr6, effective floor ~1–3%
- Dualistic system
