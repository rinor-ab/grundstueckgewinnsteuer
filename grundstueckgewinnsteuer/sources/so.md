# Solothurn (SO) – Official Sources

## Tariff & Calculation
- **Cantonal information page**: https://so.ch/verwaltung/finanzdepartement/kantonales-steueramt/grundstueckgewinnsteuer/
- **Kantonsblatt ESTV**: https://www.estv2.admin.ch/stp/kb/so-de.pdf
- **StG SO**: Legal basis

## Key Parameters
- Progressive tariff based on income tax schedule for singles
- First CHF 10'000: tax-free (Steuerfreibetrag)
- Gains > CHF 310'000: proportional 10.5%
- **No surcharges** for short holding (unlike most cantons)
- Discount: 2%/year from full 5th year, max 50% (at 30 years)

## Tax Model
- Steuerfuss model: einfache Steuer × canton + commune Steuerfuss
- Dualistic system (private property only)
