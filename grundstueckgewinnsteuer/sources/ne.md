# Neuenburg (NE) – Official Sources

## Tariff & Calculation
- **ne.ch**: https://www.ne.ch/autorites/DFS/SCCO/impots/Pages/gains-immobiliers.aspx

## Key Parameters
- Progressive brackets: 10% (≤5k), 15% (5k–10k), 20% (10k–30k), 25% (30k–50k), 30% (50k–75k), 35% (75k–100k), 40% (100k–135k), 33% (>135k)
- Min gain: CHF 5'000 (Freigrenze, below 5k not taxed)
- Surcharge: <4yr
- Discount: 6%/yr from yr5, max 60% (at yr14+)
