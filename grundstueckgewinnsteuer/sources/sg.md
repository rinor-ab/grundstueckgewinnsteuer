# St. Gallen (SG) – Official Sources

## Tariff & Calculation
- **Cantonal information page**: https://www.sg.ch/steuern-finanzen/steuern/grundstueckgewinnsteuer.html
- **Kantonsblatt ESTV**: https://www.estv2.admin.ch/stp/kb/sg-de.pdf
- **StG SG Art. 130–141**: Legal basis for Grundstückgewinnsteuer

## Key Parameters
- 12-bracket progressive tariff (Art. 140 StG)
- First CHF 2'200 tax-free
- Brackets from 0.5% to 10%; additional 10.5%/11% for gains above 248k
- Gains ≥ CHF 600'000: flat 10% on entire gain (special rule)
- Surcharges: +5% (yr1), +4% (yr2), +3% (yr3), +2% (yr4), +1% (yr5) — additive percentage points to tax rate
- Discounts from 17th year: -1.5%/year (gain < 500k), -1%/year (gain ≥ 500k)

## Tax Model
- Steuerfuss model: einfache Steuer × Steuerfuss
- State Steuerfuss 2024: 325% including supplements

## Special
- SG surcharges are **additive percentage points**, not multiplicative factors on the tax
- Gains ≥ 600k override the bracket computation entirely → flat 10%
- Two-tier discount system based on gain amount threshold (500k)
