## Key Parameters
- Linear flat rate: 30% of gain
- Minimum taxable gain: CHF 3'000
- Gain rounded down to nearest CHF 500
- Surcharges: <1yr +35%, <2yr +20%, <3yr +10%, <4yr +5%
- Discount: 2.5%/yr from yr10, max 50% (at 30 years)
