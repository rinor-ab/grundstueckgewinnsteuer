# Glarus (GL) – Official Sources

## Tariff & Calculation
- **Kantonsblatt ESTV**: https://www.estv2.admin.ch/stp/kb/gl-de.pdf
- **StG GL**: Legal basis

## Key Parameters
- Progressive brackets: 10% (first 5k), 15% (next 5k), 20% (next 5k), 25% (next 5k), 30% (above 20k)
- Minimum taxable gain: CHF 5'000 (Freigrenze — entire gain taxed if above)
- Surcharges: <1yr +30%, <2yr +20%, <3yr +15%, <4yr +10%
- Discounts: yr5 -5%, yr6 -8%, +3%/yr → max 90% at 30+ years
- Canton-level tax
