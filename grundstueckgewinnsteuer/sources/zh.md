- **Cantonal information page**: https://www.zh.ch/de/steuern-finanzen/steuern/grundstueckgewinnsteuer.html
- **Kantonsblatt ESTV**: https://www.estv2.admin.ch/stp/kb/zh-de.pdf
- **§ 227 StG ZH**: Progressive tariff table for Grundstückgewinnsteuer

## Key Parameters
- 7-bracket progressive tariff: 10% (first CHF 4,000) to 40% (above CHF 100,000)
- Minimum taxable gain: CHF 5,000 (gains below this are tax-free)
- Surcharges: <1 year +50%, 1-2 years +25%
- Discounts: from 5 full years, ~3%/year, max 50% at 20+ years

## Tax Model
- Communal-uniform: the tariff is set by the canton and applies identically in all communes
- No separate canton/commune Steuerfuss split
- Church tax is not part of GGSt in ZH
