# Freiburg (FR) – Official Sources

## Tariff & Calculation
- **fr.ch**: https://www.fr.ch/dfin/scc/grundstueckgewinnsteuer
- **DStG FR**: Legal basis

## Key Parameters
- Degressive flat rate by holding period: 22% (≤2yr) → 10% (>15yr)
- Minimum taxable gain: CHF 6'000
- Commune surcharge: 60% of canton tax (60 Rp./Fr.)
- Special surcharge: +40% on gains >400k from properties held <5yr
