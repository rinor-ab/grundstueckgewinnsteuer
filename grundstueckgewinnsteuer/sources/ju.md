# Jura (JU) – Official Sources

## Tariff & Calculation
- **jura.ch**: https://www.jura.ch/DFI/CTR/Impots/Gains-immobiliers.html
- **LI JU**: Legal basis

## Key Parameters
- Progressive brackets: 3.5% (4k–50k), 4.5% (50k–100k), 5.5% (100k–200k), 6% (200k+)
- Minimum taxable gain: CHF 4'000
- Surcharge: <2yr +50%, 2–5yr +25%
- Discount: 1%/yr from yr10
