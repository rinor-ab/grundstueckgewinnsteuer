# Graubünden (GR) – Official Sources

## Tariff & Calculation
- **Kantonsblatt ESTV**: https://www.estv2.admin.ch/stp/kb/gr-de.pdf
- **StG GR**: Legal basis for Grundstückgewinnsteuer

## Key Parameters
- Progressive brackets in CHF 9'100 steps
- Starts at 5% for first CHF 9'100, increases 1% per step up to 25% (max bracket)
- Gains above the last bracket: flat 15% top rate
- Minimum taxable gain: CHF 4'200
- Surcharge for <2 years: 2% per month under 24 months (up to 48%)
- Discount from year 10: 1.5%/year, max 51% (at ~44 years)

## Tax Model
- Canton + commune share (Steuerfuss model)
- No church tax in GGSt
