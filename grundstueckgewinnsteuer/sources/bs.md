# Basel-Stadt (BS) – Official Sources

## Tariff & Calculation
- **Cantonal information page**: https://www.steuerverwaltung.bs.ch/grundstueckgewinnsteuer.html
- **Kantonsblatt ESTV**: https://www.estv2.admin.ch/stp/kb/bs-de.pdf
- **StG BS**: Legal basis for Grundstückgewinnsteuer (revised 2023)

## Key Parameters (gültig ab 1. Januar 2023)
- Degressive flat rate by holding period with **two schedules**:
  - **Not self-used**: 60% (≤5yr), -3.9pp/yr (6–15yr), -0.9pp/yr (16–24yr), floor 12% (25+yr)
  - **Self-used**: 30% (≤5yr), -0.9pp/yr (6–24yr), floor 12% (25+yr)
- Gain reduction: 3%/year from year 6, max 60% at year 25+
- Minimum taxable gain: CHF 500
- Monistisches System (private and business assets)

## Tax Model
- Holding-period-based flat rate (two schedules)
- No per-commune Steuerfuss (BS is a single commune)
- No church tax in GGSt

## Special
- BS distinguishes between **self-used** and **non-self-used** properties
- The 2023 revision integrated the holding period discount directly into the rate schedule
