# Uri (UR) – Official Sources

## Tariff & Calculation
- **StG UR**: Legal basis
- **Kantonsblatt ESTV**: https://www.estv2.admin.ch/stp/kb/ur-de.pdf

## Key Parameters
- Progressive brackets (4% to 35%)
- Rate decreases 1%/yr with holding → 31% at <1yr down to 11% at 20yr+
- Minimum taxable gain: CHF 7'000 (Freigrenze)
- Freibetrag: CHF 10'000 deducted once per year
- Gain rounded down to CHF 100
- Monistic system
