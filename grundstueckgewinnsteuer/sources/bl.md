- **Cantonal information page**: https://www.baselland.ch/politik-und-behorden/direktionen/finanz-und-kirchendirektion/steuerverwaltung/grundstueckgewinnsteuer

## Key Parameters
- Formula-based progressive rate by gain amount (not bracket-style):
  - Base rate: 3%
  - Up to CHF 30'000: +0.03% per CHF 100 → max 12%
  - CHF 30'000–70'000: +0.02% per CHF 100 → max 20%
  - CHF 70'000–120'000: +0.01% per CHF 100 → max 25%
  - Above CHF 120'000: flat 25%
- No minimum taxable gain
- Surcharge: 1⅔% per month short of 60 months (max ~100%)
- Self-use discount: after 20+ years, CHF 5'000/year reduction, max CHF 50'000
- Monistisches System (private and business assets)

## Tax Model
- Formula-based rate applied to entire gain (not marginal brackets)
- Surcharge for short holding, gain reduction for long self-use

## Special
- BL rate formula is **applied to the whole gain**, not marginally per bracket
- Surcharge formula: `(60 - months) × 1⅔%` of the computed tax
