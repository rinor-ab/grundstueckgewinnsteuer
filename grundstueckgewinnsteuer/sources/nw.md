# Nidwalden (NW) – Official Sources

## Tariff & Calculation
- **steuern-nw.ch**: https://www.steuern-nw.ch/grundstueckgewinnsteuer
- **StG NW**: Legal basis

## Key Parameters
- Degressive flat rate by holding period (no progressive brackets)
- Rate schedule: 36% (<1yr) → 12% (30+ yrs)
- No separate surcharge/discount — built into rate table
- Monistic system (private + business property)
- Canton collects, 50% goes to commune
