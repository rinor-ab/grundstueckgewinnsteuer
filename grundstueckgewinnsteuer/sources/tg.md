# Thurgau (TG) – Official Sources

## Tariff & Calculation
- **Cantonal information page**: https://www.steuerverwaltung.tg.ch/grundstueckgewinnsteuer
- **Kantonsblatt ESTV**: https://www.estv2.admin.ch/stp/kb/tg-de.pdf
- **StG TG**: Legal basis

## Key Parameters
- Proportional base rate: **40%** of gain
- Surcharges for short holding:
  - <1yr: +50%, <2yr: +40%, <3yr: +30%, <4yr: +25%, <5yr: +20%
- Discounts for long holding:
  - 6yr: -4%, 7yr: -8%, ..., 10yr: -20%, 15yr: -40%, 20yr: -60%, 23+yr: -72%
  - Discount is 4%/yr from yr6–10, then 4%/yr from yr10–23 (capped at 72%)
- No minimum taxable gain specified
- No progressive brackets (flat proportional rate)

## Tax Model
- Flat 40% proportional rate, adjusted by holding-period surcharge/discount
- Commune/canton split via Steuerfuss
