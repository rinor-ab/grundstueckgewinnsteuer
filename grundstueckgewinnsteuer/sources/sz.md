# Schwyz (SZ) – Official Sources

## Tariff & Calculation
- **Kantonsblatt ESTV**: https://www.estv2.admin.ch/stp/kb/sz-de.pdf
- **StG SZ**: Legal basis

## Key Parameters
- Progressive bracket tariff: 8% (first 3k) → 30% (above 40k)
- Minimum taxable gain: CHF 2'000 (Freibetrag)
- Surcharges: <1yr +40%, <2yr +30%, <3yr +20%, <4yr +10%
- Discounts: yr5 -10%, then +3%/yr, max 70% (at 25 years)
- Canton-level tax (no commune split for GGSt)
