# Bern (BE) – Official Sources

## Tariff & Calculation
- **Cantonal information page**: https://www.be.ch/de/start/themen/steuern/grundstueckgewinnsteuer.html
- **Kantonsblatt ESTV**: https://www.estv2.admin.ch/stp/kb/be-de.pdf
- **StG BE Art. 142ff**: Legal basis for Grundstückgewinnsteuer

## Key Parameters
- 8-bracket progressive tariff: 1.44% (first CHF 2,700) to 8.10% (above CHF 263,100)
- Minimum taxable gain: CHF 5,200 (tax-free below)
- Surcharges: <1yr +70%, 1-2yr +50%, 2-3yr +35%, 3-4yr +20%, 4-5yr +10%
- Discounts: from 6th year, 2%/year reduction of taxable gain (not tax), max 70% at 40+ years
