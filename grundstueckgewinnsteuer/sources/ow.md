# Obwalden (OW) – Official Sources

## Tariff & Calculation
- **ow.ch**: https://www.ow.ch/verwaltung/finanzdepartement/steuerverwaltung/grundstueckgewinnsteuer
- **StG OW**: Legal basis

## Key Parameters
- Proportional: 2% × Steuerfuss (canton 3.15 + commune varies)
- Minimum taxable gain: CHF 5'000
- Surcharge for <3 years holding (rate varies)
- No discount for long holding
