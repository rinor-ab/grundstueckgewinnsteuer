# Tessin (TI) – Official Sources

## Tariff & Calculation
- **ti.ch**: https://www4.ti.ch/dfp/dc/sportello/calcolatori/utili-immobiliari
- **LT TI art. 123–140**: Legal basis

## Key Parameters
- Degressive flat rate by holding period: 31% (≤1yr) → 4% (30+yr)
- No separate surcharge/discount — built into rate schedule
- Monistic system
